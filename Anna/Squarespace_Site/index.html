Welcome to Freedman & Katz Psychotherapy, PLLC, where we offer warm, compassionate therapy backed by evidence-based practices to help you navigate life’s joys and struggles. Whether you’re feeling a bit overwhelmed by stress or anxiety, tangled in interpersonal concerns, mourning a loss, or trying to find your paws through life changes, we’re here to offer warm, compassionate care with a purrsonal touch. Our goal is to provide a safe, understanding space where you can land softly and find your way forward with confidence. Let’s work together to turn those rough patches into smoother, more fulfilling paths—one step at a time.
